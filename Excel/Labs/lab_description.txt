**Excel Labs**  

Welcome to the Excel Labs folder!  
This section of the repository is dedicated to showcasing various hands-on exercises and projects that demonstrate the use of Excel for data analysis and visualization.  

**Lab Overview**  
The labs are designed to enhance your understanding of Excel features and how they can be applied to solve real-world problems.  

**How to Use This Repository**  
--Navigate to the specific lab folder (e.g., Lab 1 - Conditional Formatting).  
--Open the Excel file to view the exercises.  
--Follow the instructions provided in the file to complete the tasks.

**Why Excel?**  
--Excel is a powerful tool for  
--Data organization  
--Analysis and visualization  
--Automating repetitive tasks with formulas and macros  

These labs will help you master essential skills to make the most of Excel in any data-related task.
