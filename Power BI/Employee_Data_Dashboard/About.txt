**Employee Dashboard**
This Power BI dashboard provides an overview of employee data, offering insights into workforce demographics, performance metrics, and organizational trends.

--Features
  Demographics: Gender, age distribution, and department-wise breakdown.
  Performance Metrics: Employee performance ratings and growth trends.
  Insights: Attrition rates, tenure analysis, and key workforce trends.

--How to Use
  Download the .pbix file from this repository.
  Open it in Power BI Desktop.
I Interact with slicers and filters to explore the data.
